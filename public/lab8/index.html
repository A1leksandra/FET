Tips & Techniques

Seasonal Lawn Care Tips

Fall

Autumn is the perfect time to prepare your lawn for winter. Rake leaves regularly, aerate the soil, and apply fertilizer to strengthen roots before the cold season.

Spring

Spring awakening requires careful attention. Remove winter debris, overseed bare spots, and begin regular mowing as grass starts growing actively.

Summer

Summer maintenance focuses on proper watering, mowing at correct heights, and protecting grass from heat stress with adequate hydration.
